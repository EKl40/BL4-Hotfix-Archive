Minor Update Notes - March 5, 2026

This Minor Update includes Pearlescent drop rate changes and QOL updates to the content released in Bounty Pack 2. 

Please restart your game to ensure that the update goes through and to prevent connection errors in matchmaking.

If you're encountering any issues, please contact 2K Support for direct assistance. You can also read the PC Troubleshooting Guide, a list of known issues, or browse a list of Support threads here.
Change Notes:

    Updated the Pearlescent Eigenburst to drop at its intended rate
    The scaling on Pearlescent drops in Ultimate Vault Hunter levels has been adjusted
        Dev Note: We have adjusted how often Pearlescent gear appears at higher Ultimate Vault Hunter levels to meet our goals for this top-tier rarity. The starting drop chance remains the same, but the way it scales as you level up has been tuned.
    Standardized dedicated loot reward chances across all bosses in the base game, Bounty Packs, and Invincible encounters
        Dev Note: With a new rarity now appearing in the wild, we’ve updated how bosses drop their loot to make things simpler. Bosses in the base game (including Invincibles) and all Bounty Packs now use the same drop rate for their dedicated items. This change does not impact Phosophene drop rates. We are happy to bring this update to your loot hunt! 
    Reduced the health of all Ordonite enemies’ spawned Clones by 25%. The final boss has been unchanged
    Ordonite Cannisters now have more weight to them to create a softer flight and landing. We have also added extra glow to make them easier to see
